  This HALO Micro-Fleece Sleepsack Swaddle is the perfect blend of comfort and safety for your little one. The 3-way adjustable design lets you swaddle your baby's arms in, hands-to-face, or one or both arms out, giving you control over their sleep style. Made from premium 100% cotton fabric, it's gentle against delicate skin and free from harsh chemicals. The two-way zipper with zipper covers at the top and bottom ensures easy access and secure fitting. With its 'Back is Best' embroidery, this swaddle is designed to promote safe sleep practices by reminding caregivers to place the baby on their back. It's also recognized as 'Hip Healthy' by the International Hip Dysplasia Institute, making it a reliable choice for parents focused on their baby's well-being.

Its generous sack design allows for natural kicking movements while keeping the baby secure and warm throughout the night. Designed for use over regular sleepwear, this swaddle replaces the need for loose blankets, ensuring a safe and cozy sleeping environment for your baby.
